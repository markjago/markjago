Private Tutoring

I offer private tuition to a small number of philosophy and logic students, on a one-to-one basis.

For each student, I put together a unique package of tuition, based on their aims and needs, whether this be:

  • Passing an upcoming exam
  • Progressing with a university or adult-education course
  • Developing a general interest

For students looking for a general introduction to philosophy, we might cover a range of philosophical topics, at a level appropriate to your prior knowledge and skills.

For students working to a fixed syllabus or wanting to develop a specific interest, I can put together a targeted set of materials to investigate those topics in depth.

Specific topics we may cover include:

  • Philosophy of mind and consciousness
  • Metaphysics
  • Epistemology (the theory of knowledge)
  • Philosophy of Science
  • Philosophy of language
  • Theories of truth and meaning
  • Formal or informal Logic

Specific ‘from the canon’ philosophers we might cover include (although I’m open to pother suggestions!):

  • Descartes
  • Locke
  • Wittgenstein
  • Frege and Russell

If you’d like more information about one-to-one tuition with me, please email me at mark.jago@nottingham.ac.uk.
